Frequently Asked Questions

How do I mark my attendance? +

You can mark your attendance through the Attendance System page. Click on "Mark Attendance" and confirm your presence for the day.

How do I apply for leave? +

Navigate to the Leave Management section, fill out the leave application form with your dates and reason, and submit for approval.

How can I change my room? +

Room changes can be requested through the Room Management section. Submit a room change request with your preferred room number and reason.
